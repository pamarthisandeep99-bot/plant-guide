Basic Care Rules

Follow these simple rules to keep your plants happy.

1

Don't Overwater

More plants die from overwatering than underwatering. Always check the top inch of soil before watering.

2

Right Light

Match your plant to your light conditions. Not all plants can survive in dark corners or direct sun.

3

Good Drainage

Always use pots with drainage holes to prevent root rot and ensure healthy growth.
